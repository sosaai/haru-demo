QUESTIONS WE ARE OFTEN ASKED

Good to know.

Q · 01
Can I order à la carte?

Yes — Tuesday to Friday at the first seating only. Saturday and the second seating are omakase only.

Q · 02
How far in advance should I book?

Reservations open thirty days in advance, 09:00 Milan time. The Hoshi counter is typically full within four days; Tenku (Saturdays) within the first hour.

Q · 03
Do you accept walk-ins?

Rarely — only if a guest is delayed by more than 20 minutes. It is worth calling at 19:15.

Q · 04
Can you accommodate allergies and dietary restrictions?

Most — we ask for at least 48 hours' notice. Shellfish, sesame, and gluten can be removed in full. We do not currently offer a vegetarian omakase.

Q · 05
Is there a dress code?

No code, only a polite request — no strong perfume or cologne, so the fish has space to speak.

Q · 06
Can I take photographs?

Yes, of your pieces. Please ask the chef before photographing him at work — he will usually nod.
